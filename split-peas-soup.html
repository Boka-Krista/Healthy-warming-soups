Split peas soup

  • 800g smoked ribs
  • 400g yellow split peas
  • 2 bayleafs
  • 6 potatoes
  • pot for soup
  • pinch of salt
  • black pepper
  • 2 cubes of vegetable stock
  • 2 meduim or 1 large carrot
  • 2 medium or 1 large onion
  1. First you put meat to boil along with split peas for 1 to 1.5 hours.
  2. While everything is boiling, peel potatoes and cut them into small squares.
  3. peel carrot(s) and onion(s)
  4. grate the carrot(s) and chop the onion(s) into small pieces, then fry them for couple minutes until softened, add to boiling soup.
  5. When the meat is ready, cut into small pieces (you can also just sred it).
  6. When all done, put everything back to pot on small gas to boil to another 15-20 mins.
  7. Enjoy with sour cream or with freshly cut parsley. Of course, it's also delishious without this extras.
