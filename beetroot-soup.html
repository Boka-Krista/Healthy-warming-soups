Beetroot soup

  • 800g smoked ribs
  • 6 beetroots
  • 325g pearl barley
  • 6 potatoes
  • pot for soup
  • pinch of salt
  • black pepper
  • 2 cubes of vegetable stock
  • 2 bayleafs

  1. First you put meat to boil along with beetroot and barley for couple of hours, depending on size, beetroot usually is boiled within an hour, when so, put them aside to cool a bit.
  2. While everything is boiling, peel potatoes and cut them into small squares.
  3. Then add bayleaf, stock, salt and pepper
  4. Once the meat is ready, put it aside to cool little bit, while you peel and grate the beetroots
  5. When the meat has cooled, cut into small pieces (you can also just sred it).
  6. When all done, put everything back to pot on small gas to boil to another 15-20 mins.
  7. Enjoy with sour cream or with freshly cut parsley. Of course, it's also delishious without this extras.
