  • 800g pork belly
  • 2 jars of souerkraut
  • 6 potatoes
  • pot for soup
  • pinch of salt
  • black pepper
  1. First you put meat to boil for about 1.5 hours.
  2. Add souerkraut and keep boiling.
  3. Peel potatoes and cut in bigger cubes.
  4. Add spices.
  5. Boil another approcimate 20 mins or until potatoes are softened.
  6. Enjoy!
